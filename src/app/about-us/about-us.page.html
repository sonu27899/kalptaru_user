About Us


Kalptaru Furniture build custom furniture according to your specifications. Its design experts understand your idea to share a workable 3D for exact specifications & confirmation. It offer free consultation, to help you select the best wood & finishes to complement your furniture designs.




It takes a special touch & Imagination to custom design your furniture. It understand that furniture no longer attributes for “one size fits all”, so it offer an endless array of customization options to make sure the furniture is perfect for your home space! Crafting each unit with premium quality & best solid wood we ensure a lasting durability. It offer furniture for every budget!




In an effort to help customers make an informed decision whilst buying furniture, Kalptaru Furniture displays its furniture in ‘actual room settings’ viz. the furniture with furnishings & accessories are displayed in actual room layouts having different themes. Each room showcases a different mood with color coordinated accessories & wall textures to beautifully compliment them. The settings on display are for living, offices, dining & bedroom. Realistic room settings enable the customers to visualize the furniture in their home, making buying decisions easier.
